How it works

Three steps, then it runs itself.

No forms, no setup, no learning curve. The work is in the talking — the app does the sorting.

STEP 01

Speak, or type

Tap the mic and brain-dump. On-device speech means it works offline, and your raw words never have to leave the phone.

STEP 02

It gets structured

Each intention becomes a task with a due date, a priority and — for anything daunting — tiny first steps. Observations stay as notes, not fake to-dos.

STEP 03

Share & delegate

Drop it into a shared space — a household, a co-parenting pair, a care team — and it syncs in real time to everyone who needs it, with a record of who did what.

Who it's for

Tools built for exactly how coordination fails today.

The same capture-and-share core adapts to four kinds of load — pick what fits, and the app tunes itself around it.

🧠

Focus & ADHD

For minds that overflow

When a list feels impossible and starting is the hardest part.

  • Voice brain-dump — get it all out, let the app sort it
  • “Just one thing” to quiet the overwhelm
  • Break any task into two-minute first steps
  • No red, no nagging — unfinished tasks just wait, kindly
🤝

Co-parenting

One clear source of truth

For two households that need calm, provable coordination.

  • Custody days — whose day it is, with a handover heads-up
  • Shared expense ledger: who paid, who owes
  • Tone check flags hostile wording before it lands
  • Append-only history neither parent can rewrite — export any time
💙

Caregiving

A care team, in sync

For families coordinating care for a parent, relative or friend.

  • Shared profile of the person everyone cares for
  • Medications & routines — every tick a record, misses flagged
  • Voice care journal and a “since your last visit” handoff
  • One-tap doctor briefing from weeks of notes
👪

Households & families

Share the mental load

For the person holding the family's whole to-do list in their head.

  • One shared list that updates on everyone's phone
  • Delegate a task; each person sees just what's theirs
  • Who added and who did what, with the time
  • A once-a-day digest of what's open and what got done

What's inside

Small app. Serious depth.

🎙 On-device voice

Speech-to-text that can run entirely on the phone — private and offline.

🪄 AI that structures

Dates, priorities, tags and step-by-step breakdowns, with a built-in fallback when AI is off.

🔄 Real-time spaces

Multiple shared lists at once; tasks sync live and work offline, then reconcile.

🔐 Accounts & sync

Sign in with Google or email to carry your spaces across every device.

📜 Append-only records

Shared history that can't be quietly edited — the backbone of trust between co-parents and carers.

♿ Built for access

Quiet visual mode, big tap targets, full keyboard and screen-reader support, reduced-motion aware.

Trust by design

Yours, portable, and honest about your data.

● Private by default

Ideas and voice transcripts stay on your device; only tasks you share are synced.

● Consent-first analytics

Anonymous usage only, opt-in — never the words you type, names, or amounts.

● Can't be rewritten

Shared history is append-only, so a record stays a record for co-parents and carers.

● Yours to leave with

Export any time as CSV or PDF. Works offline. No lock-in.
