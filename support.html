Support

Quick fixes for the common things, and how to reach a human.

Reach us

The fastest channel is a GitHub issue — describe what you expected and what happened instead. Please never post screenshots that show other people's names or your tasks; the app's Settings → Developer functions → Copy diagnostics gives us what we need with no content in it.

Quick fixes

"My captures say built-in instead of ai"

Open Settings → Developer functions → Test AI. It checks the whole chain and tells you which step is failing, with the fix in the message. The most common cause: an old pasted API key overriding the hosted service — clear it in Settings → Smart AI. Second most common: the fair-use daily AI limit (it resets overnight UTC).

"The mic doesn't transcribe"

Check the browser has microphone permission for the app. On iOS Safari, live word-by-word transcription isn't available — recordings transcribe in short segments instead, which needs a connection. For fully offline, private transcription, enable the on-device model in Settings → Private on-device voice recognition.

"My space isn't syncing to the other person"

Both devices need to be signed in (Settings → Account) and members of the same space — check Settings → Spaces on both. Changes sync when a device is online; the app works offline and catches up. If a space you expect is missing after switching devices, sign in with the same account and it restores.

"I got a new phone"

If you signed in with Google or email: install the app, sign in, and your spaces restore. Anonymous accounts live on the old device — link an email or Google sign-in in Settings → Account before switching, or use Settings → Your data → Export backup / Import backup to carry everything across manually.

"I want my data out"

Settings → Your data → Export backup gives you everything as JSON. Every space's record also exports as CSV, and every report prints to PDF. Export is free forever — that's a product promise, not a tier.

"I want to delete everything"

Two separate things: Settings → Your data → Delete all data wipes this device. Settings → Account → Delete account deletes your account and access on the server — see the privacy policy for exactly what that removes and what remains with your former spaces.

Known limitations

  • Task extraction is English-only today; other languages are kept safely as notes.
  • Exact-minute alarms need the upcoming store app; the web app promises records and schedules, not alarms.
  • The product is in active development — see the product overview for what's shipped versus planned.
