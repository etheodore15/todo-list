Privacy policy

Effective 21 July 2026 · applies to the Idea → Todo app and website

The short version. Your tasks and notes live on your device. Nothing is uploaded unless you deliberately use a feature that needs it — creating or joining a shared space, signing in, or using Smart AI. We never sell data, never show ads, and never paywall getting your data out. You can export everything and delete your account yourself, in the app.

Who we are

Idea → Todo is operated by its developer ("we", "the operator") in Australia. Contact options are on the support page.

What stays on your device

By default, everything: tasks, captured notes and transcripts, your private journal, photos you attach, settings, and any API key you paste. It is stored in your browser's local storage on your device and goes nowhere unless a feature below sends it somewhere at your request. Deleting the app's data (Settings → Your data → Delete all data) removes it.

What leaves your device, and when

  • Shared spaces (optional). When you create or join a space, the content of that space — its tasks, shared notes, the append-only history, expenses, receipt photos, and member display names — syncs through our hosted database (Google Firebase / Cloud Firestore, project todo-list-50050, hosted on Google Cloud in the United States). Access is enforced by server-side security rules: only signed-in members of a space can read it. Your private journal and unshared tasks are never synced.
  • Your account (optional). Sign-in is anonymous by default — an ID with no name or email. If you choose Google or email sign-in, we store the email address, linked to the same account. We also keep a small per-account record: your list of space memberships, your onboarding cohort selection, and a daily Smart AI usage counter (a number only — never content).
  • Smart AI (optional). When Smart AI structures a capture, breaks a task down, tone-checks a message, enriches a report, or reads text from a photo, the relevant text or image is sent to Google's Gemini API — through our proxy server (which checks your daily quota and forwards the request; it does not store your content), or directly with your own key if you pasted one. Google processes it to return the result, under Google's API terms. Turn Smart AI off, or never enable it, and the built-in engine does the work entirely on your device.
  • Voice (optional). Live transcription uses your browser's speech engine, which on most browsers sends audio to the browser vendor's servers (e.g. Google's, on Chrome). Where the browser has no engine, short recorded segments are transcribed via the Smart AI path above. The opt-in on-device model (Whisper) transcribes entirely on your device and works offline — nothing leaves it.
  • Analytics (off by default). If we ever enable analytics, it is opt-in, anonymous, and feature-level only — which buttons get used, never what you typed. No task, note, name, or amount is ever sent. Off unless you turn it on; change your mind in Settings any time.

What we never do

  • No selling or sharing of personal information with third parties for their purposes.
  • No advertising, no trackers, no fingerprinting.
  • No reading your content. The operator's dashboard sees counts and feature usage, not what you wrote. Shared-space content is readable only by that space's members.
  • No paywall on your data: export (CSV, print/PDF, full JSON backup) is free, forever.

Shared spaces are shared

A space's history is deliberately append-only — that's the trust layer that makes the record worth keeping. Content you put into a shared space is visible to that space's members and carries your display name. If you later leave a space or delete your account, the record you contributed stays with the space's remaining members, the way sent messages stay with their recipients. Your name label remains on those entries; your account and its access are gone.

Retention and deletion

  • Device data: yours, on your device; delete it in Settings → Your data.
  • Account: delete it yourself in Settings → Account → Delete account. This removes your sign-in, your per-account records (space list, cohort selection), and your access to every space. Server backups age out within about 35 days.
  • AI quota counters (a daily number, no content) are deleted with the account or age out naturally.

Children

The app is built for household coordination and may be used by families together, but accounts are for people 13 and over (or the age of digital consent where you live). Younger children should participate through a parent's or guardian's space.

Security

Hosted data is protected by Google Cloud's infrastructure security, transport encryption (HTTPS), and per-space membership rules enforced on the server and covered by automated tests in our public repository. No system is perfect; we run with the smallest data footprint we can so there is less to protect.

Your rights

We handle personal information in line with the Australian Privacy Principles. Wherever you are, you can access your data (export, in-app), correct it (edit, in-app), and delete it (in-app). For anything else — including complaints, or questions this page doesn't answer — use the support page. If you're in Australia and unsatisfied with our response, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (oaic.gov.au).

Changes

If this policy changes materially, the effective date above changes and the app's release notes say so. We won't quietly weaken it.
