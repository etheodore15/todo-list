Support

Quick fixes for the common things, and how to reach a human. Pilot circles also have their direct line — use it.

Reach us

The fastest channel is a GitHub issue — describe what you expected and what happened instead. Please never post screenshots showing a participant's name or any circle content; the app's Settings → Developer functions → Copy diagnostics gives us what we need with no content in it.

Quick fixes

"My captures say built-in instead of ai"

Open Settings → Developer functions → Test AI. It checks the whole chain and names the failing step, with the fix in the message. Most common: an old pasted API key overriding the hosted service (clear it in Settings → Smart AI), or the fair-use daily AI limit.

"A worker can't see something I posted"

That's usually visibility working as designed: incident reports are only visible to their author and the circle's admins, and "team"-scoped items don't show to workers. The circle's owner sees everything, always. Roles are listed in Settings → Spaces → the circle.

"The circle isn't syncing to someone"

Both devices need to be signed in (Settings → Account) and members of the circle — check on both. Changes sync when online; the app works offline and catches up. After switching devices, sign in with the same account and memberships restore.

"I got a new phone"

If you signed in with Google or email: install the app, sign in, and your circles restore. Anonymous accounts live on the old device — link a sign-in in Settings → Account before switching, or use Settings → Your data → Export backup / Import backup.

"We need the record out"

Every circle's history exports as CSV, the ledger exports claim-shaped columns, the Plan Review Pack prints to PDF, and Settings → Your data → Export backup takes everything as JSON. Export is free forever — that's a product promise. A circle that's ending can be archived, or exported and removed.

"I want to delete everything"

Two separate things: Settings → Your data → Delete all data wipes this device. Settings → Account → Delete account deletes your account and access on the server — the privacy policy explains exactly what's removed and what stays in your former circles' records.

Known limitations

  • Task extraction is English-only today; other languages are kept safely as notes.
  • Exact-minute alarms need the future store app; the web app promises records and schedules, not alarms.
  • Cooee is in pilot — see the product overview for what's shipped versus planned.
